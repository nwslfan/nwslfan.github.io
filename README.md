# ⚽ NWSL Fantasy League

Pick the winners. Climb the leaderboard. Brag to your friends.

Live at: **https://nwslfan.github.io**

---

## What is it?

A weekly pick'em league for the 2026 NWSL season. Every week, players predict the winner of each match. Points are awarded for correct picks — with a bonus for picking an underdog that most people slept on.

At the end of the season, the top 3 players split the prize pool. 💰

---

## How to play

1. **Each week**, fill out the Google Form with your match winner picks before the games start
2. **Watch the games** 📺
3. **Check the leaderboard** to see how you stack up against everyone else

---

## Scoring ⭐

| | Points |
|---|---|
| ✅ Correct pick | +1 |
| 🌟 Correct underdog pick (fewer than 30% of players chose them) | +1 bonus |
| 🎯 Closest guess on total goals scored | noted on leaderboard |

---

## Prize pool 🏆

Every player puts in $5. Payouts go to the top 3 at the end of the season:
- 🥇 1st place — 60%
- 🥈 2nd place — 30%
- 🥉 3rd place — 10%
